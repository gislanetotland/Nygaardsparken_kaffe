Nygårdsparken Kaffé Kollektiv is part of Kommunens Ny Chance work training measures Kafe Mat & prat. The work is led by Odd Erik Dahl who is the activity coordinator in the new farm park together with experts chefs who provide participants with theoretical and practical training in working on kitchens and canteens. The training is also supported by a Norwegian teacher.

Nomads Kitchen

Nomads Kitchen was born in 2019 out of my passion to create a magical delicious synergy and stories around my food with the local community of Bergen, to create and inspire memories and nostalgia which could be in a sustainable and edible bowl made of bread holding a cheesy comforting soup or a plate of Baked Potatoes with raclette or a mexican inspired tostada with homemade pickles. Or perhaps in New York style chewy Bagels with fillings from around the globe based on my memories and experiences.

Then there is also my homemade cakes or hot chocolate which bring waves of koselig warmth and have already won many hearts. On the other hand my Wienerbrød requires intense technical knowledge and patience to laminate a flaky pastry which takes two days to make and acts as a platform to hold a delicious array of flavours based on my interpretation and creativity. I am constantly innovating and creating to offer something unique which you will not easily find in Bergen, which only Nomads Kitchen has to offer, homemade with love.

My kitchen is my canvas and butter and flour or vegetables and beans are my tools with which I create the foundations for comfy soulful bites or a piece of flavourful textural art inspired by my travels and memories. To create my dishes a lot of planning, experimenting and thought goes behind the scenes, to create new flavours and dishes. A lot of sourcing of quality ingredients from all over town which are available locally and can help manifest my vision through my food.

Why Nomads Kitchen? I was born a Nomad, an explorer and traveller who has always been on the move to live and learn and my next goal dream is to travel around Norway with my kitchen and table to host pop ups and events to connect people through my food….

NyDeli

NyDeli was a dream come true that started after meeting with Odd Erik Dahl the activity coordinator for Nygårdsparken. He has been instrumental in giving us the opportunity to express our creativity and build a concept and been an immense pillar of strength guiding us all along the way.

My background with a masters in microbiology and work experience in quality control helped me to get an approval from Mattilsynet, the norwegian food safety authority. I received a lot of encouragement from my husband’s work colleagues who regularly ordered take away from us.

Although food and cooking has been my passion since I was a child I had formal education in Microbiology and had cooked for a maximum of 30 people before. I ended up cooking for 100 people and we had almost 150 visitors! For the first time in my life I had cleaned and prepared 10 kilos of lamb, almost 7 kilos of potatoes and 5 kilos of rice.

NyDeli has given all of us a chance to share our culture and food with the local community. We have celebrated many Indian festivals like Holi and Diwali at the park with tremendous response and participation from fellow indian migrants as well as those from many other countries and native norwegians.

What is unique about NyDeli is the fact that people always feel that they get to experience authentic Indian cuisine as they would in an Indian home and that's what appeals most to them.

Verdenskafféen

Verdenskafeen in Nygårdsparken is part of Kafe Mat & Prat. Kafe food and chatter is New Chance's work training initiative. The work is led by experts chefs who provide training in work in the kitchen and canteen. The trainees get Norwegian lessons 4 days a week, and lessons in restaurant and food subjects two days a week.

We serve exciting dishes from different continents. The menu changes each month.

We are based on the trainees' food culture, and that's what it is forms the basis of what we have on our menu in the park. We have great faith in that food provides a good arena for learning and integration. And not least, that our Interns should be proud of their culture and share their knowledge with ours guests.

Welcome to our pleasant café in the park!
